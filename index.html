THE BILL HALL
MEMORIAL FUND



Bill hall says he enjoys driving his 1957 cream Ford Fairlane through the streets of Galena, KS because the town is part of him; but some people might say it's the other way around.

"We're Galena people. That means you share your ups and downs with your friends and neighbors." He was born in Galena in 1937 in his parents'home, when, he says, "Hospitals weren't used much. The doctor would just come out to the house".
